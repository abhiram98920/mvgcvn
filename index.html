The Journey

The Path of Kalaripayattu

A progressive journey through four distinct stages of mastery — body, weapon, metal, and spirit.

Meythari

The foundation. Physical flexibility, powerful leg movements, and body control through fluid sequences that awaken dormant strength.

Kolthari

Introduction to wooden weapons. Staff, short stick, and curved wooden sword training demanding fearless coordination.

Ankathari

Mastering metal weapons. Dagger, sword, shield, and the Urumi — the whip-sword demanding complete control of body and mind.

Verumkai

The final, highest stage. Unarmed combat — using the body itself as a weapon, striking vital marma points for mastery without a blade.
