The Ancient Science

Kalari Chikitsa

Kalari massage is an integral part of the ancient martial art of Kalaripayattu and its healing system known as Kalari Chikitsa. Traditionally, this knowledge is transmitted through the Gurukula system under the direct guidance of a Gurukkal.

In the authentic tradition, Kalari massage cannot be learned through short courses. Mastering authentic Kalari massage requires at least seven years of dedicated training to be applied safely.
